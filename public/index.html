FUN FACTS

  • Lions are the only cats that live in groups.

  • A group, or pride, can be up to 30 lions, depending on how much food and water is available.

  • Female lions are the main hunters. While they’re out looking for food, the males guard the pride’s territory and their young.

  • A lion’s roar can be heard up to eight kilometres away.

  • Lions scent mark their territory, using their wee, to create a border.

OUR VALUES

Passion

Our amazing team of staff are all highly passionate about the safety & welfare of our animals, as well as sharing a common interest in wildlife. All employees are passionate to help everyone andhave no fear of working with our animals or helping our customers, If you ever need help, just locate a member of staff and they will assist you.

Dedication

Any zoo needs dedicated staff, Wilson Zoo has ensured the highering of top-quality staff with a dedicated passion to help! From feeding animals 4 times a day, to cleaning up after 20 different types, Our employees are the core to functionality of our Zoo.

Respect

We embrace different cultures, values and behaviours, but use the most up to date animal welfare research to make improvements, helping ensure harmful, outdated practices become a thing of the past, while encouraging positive cultural action for the future.
